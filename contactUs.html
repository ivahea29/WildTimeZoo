About Our Zoo

Here at North Shore Zoo we prioritise the health and happiness of the animals we care for. We provide state of the art care and attempt to emulate animals natural habitats as closely as possible to make them as comfortable and happy as possible. Aside from providing our animals with the best care we also want to provide those who visit our zoo with the best experience possible. When you buy a ticket to visit our zoo you are directly helping us care for the animals and we want to thank you for that by giving you the best experience we can! Our knowledgable tour guides can answer any questions you might have. We want you to leave our zoo at the end of the day feeling educated.
