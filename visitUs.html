Primate Habitat

Come check out our new Primate Habitat! We have carefully curated and simulated a real Jungle in which the primates can feel right at home! You will find orangatans, chimpanzee, lemurs and many more primate species to learn about.

Turtle Nursery

This is a seasonal event, but quickly book in during the spring and you will catch a rare glimpse of the baby turtles leaving their nest and become zoo residents. Please contat beforehand as bookings are limited.

Safari Kingdom

Ever wondered what the plains of the continent of Africa look like? Then look no further, as one of our oldest but most detailed enclosure in the park, with a wide range of animals for all members of the Whanau to enjoy.. With our resident lion Pride you will get a first hand experience on the inner workings of a family of lions. You will find a carefully curated experience.

Snake Island

With a number of exotic Snakes, you will find out extensive variety. An experience you will never forget! Specialy designed so all vistors can get a up close experience and if you book in for one of our tours you may even get a opportunity to hold a real life Anaconda!
